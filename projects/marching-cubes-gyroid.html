Marching Cubes: Gyroid

The Gyroid is a triply periodic minimal surface discovered by NASA scientist Alan Schoen in 1970. It's a remarkable mathematical structure with zero mean curvature and no self-intersections.

This visualization uses the Marching Cubes algorithm to generate the 3D mesh in real-time.
